Our Story

Built by students, for students.

S+Tutor.my started as a university project with a simple question: why is it so hard to find a reliable, affordable tutor in Malaysia? We were frustrated by scattered Facebook groups, inconsistent pricing, and zero accountability. So we built something better — a platform where students can discover, compare, and book verified tutors in minutes, not days.

🎯

Our Mission

To make quality tutoring accessible to every Malaysian student, regardless of location or budget — through a transparent, trusted, and easy-to-use platform.

🔭

Our Vision

To become Malaysia's most trusted tutoring marketplace, where every student achieves their academic potential and every tutor builds a sustainable, rewarding career.
